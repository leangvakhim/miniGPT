Mini-GPT

Tiny Shakespeare Dataset

Welcome! This chatbot is powered by a custom-built Transformer model, trained from scratch on the works of William Shakespeare.

Why does it sound funny?

This model predicts text character-by-character, not word-by-word. Because the dataset is tiny and it only looks at the next letter, it often invents non-existent words and sounds like a babbling child trying to speak Old English!

It was built to test and visualize core Transformer architecture concepts, focusing on sequence modeling and self-attention at the most granular (character) level.
